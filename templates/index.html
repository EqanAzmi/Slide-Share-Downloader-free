Frequently Asked Questions

How does this tool work?

Simply paste a SlideShare presentation URL, select your preferred format (PDF or PPTX), and click download. The tool will extract all slides and convert them into your chosen format.

What URLs are supported?

This tool supports public SlideShare presentations with URLs like: https://www.slideshare.net/username/presentation-name

What's the difference between PDF and PPTX?

PDF: Best for viewing and sharing. Slides are converted to high-quality images in a single document.
PPTX: Best for editing. Each slide is placed on a separate PowerPoint slide, ready for further modifications.

Why might a download fail?

Downloads may fail if the presentation is private, requires login, or if SlideShare has changed their website structure. Try refreshing the page and trying again.

Is this tool free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs or limitations on the number of downloads.
